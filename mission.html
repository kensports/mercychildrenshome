Our Mission

What We Provide
Every Single Day

We provide holistic care that nurtures every aspect of a child's development — body, mind, and spirit. This is what drives us forward.

What We Do

Six Pillars of Care

Every child in our home receives comprehensive support across all areas of life. Nothing is left out.

01

Safe Shelter

Many of our children come from situations of neglect and uncertainty. Here, they find more than just a roof — they find a place where they are safe, loved, and finally at peace.

02

Education

Every child deserves a chance to learn and dream. We provide school fees, uniforms, books, and support — giving each child the opportunity to build a brighter future.

03

Nutrition

For many of our children, regular meals were never guaranteed. Today, we provide three nourishing meals a day — restoring their strength, health, and hope.

04

Healthcare

We ensure every child receives the medical care they need — from routine checkups to urgent treatment — so they can grow up strong and healthy.

05

Spiritual Care

Beyond physical needs, we nurture the heart. Through faith, love, and guidance, we help each child discover their value and purpose in God's plan.

06

Future Skills

As they grow, we equip our children with practical skills and confidence — preparing them for independent, meaningful lives beyond the home.

Our Impact

Making a Difference Every Day

Since 2020, Mercy Children's Home has been a beacon of hope for the most vulnerable children in Kasanda Bukuya. Every day we wake up committed to the mission.

  • 32 children receive shelter, love, and care every day
  • 3 nutritious meals provided daily to every child
  • All children enrolled in school with full support
  • Regular medical checkups and healthcare access
  • Weekly faith-based programs and spiritual mentorship
  • Supported by a global community of donors and volunteers

Our Vision

A Uganda Where No Child is Left Behind

We envision a future where every orphaned and vulnerable child in Uganda has access to safety, education, and love. We are building that future one child at a time — and with your support, we can reach more families, more communities, and more lives.

Our Journey

How It All Began

2020

Mercy Children's Home is Founded

Out of a deep calling to serve vulnerable children in Kasanda Bukuya, Mercy Children's Home opened its doors for the first time — welcoming its first children with love and faith.

2021

Growing Our Family

More children found their way to the home as word spread through the community. The team expanded, and so did our programs — adding structured education support and healthcare access.

2022–2023

Reaching the World

Global supporters began partnering with the mission — from individuals to churches around the world. Every contribution helped provide more meals, more education, and more hope.

Today

32 Children & Growing

Today we care for 32 children and continue to grow. The mission remains the same — love every child, serve every need, and never stop believing in a better future for Uganda's most vulnerable.
