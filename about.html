Who We Are

A Faith-Based Family in the Heart of Uganda

Mercy Children's Home is a faith-based organisation located in Kasanda Bukuya, Uganda, dedicated to providing comprehensive care for orphaned, abandoned, and vulnerable children.

We believe every child deserves more than survival — they deserve a future filled with hope, purpose, and opportunity. Through faith, care, and community support, we are helping these children grow into strong, confident, and God-fearing individuals.

We currently support 32 children, providing them with shelter, three nutritious meals daily, education, healthcare, and most importantly — love.

"Every child who walks through our doors finds safety, love, and a community that cares. This is not just a home — it is a family."

— Mercy Children's Home

But this mission is only possible with the help of people like you.

Our Community

We are a tight-knit community of children, caregivers, volunteers, and supporters from around the world united by one goal — giving every child a chance at life.

Our Foundation

Founded in 2020, Mercy Children's Home was built on the belief that faith and compassion can transform lives. Every decision we make is guided by love and purpose.

Global Support

Supporters from across the world help make our mission possible. Every donation, prayer, and share brings us closer to reaching more children in need.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Everything we do flows from these core values that guide our team every single day.

Unconditional Love

Every child receives love without condition — regardless of their background, history, or circumstances. Love is the foundation of everything we do.

Faith & Purpose

We are a faith-driven organisation. We believe God's purpose for every child is greatness, and we work to help each child discover that truth.

Education First

We invest in every child's education because we know learning is the most powerful tool for breaking cycles of poverty and building futures.

Community

No child thrives in isolation. We build a strong community within our home and connect with the wider village of supporters around the world.

Holistic Care

We care for the whole child — body, mind, and spirit. From medical care to emotional support, every aspect of development matters to us.

Safety & Dignity

Every child deserves to feel safe and valued. We protect, respect, and honour the dignity of every child in our home, always.

Where We Are

Kasanda Bukuya, Uganda

We are based in Kasanda Bukuya, a rural district in central Uganda. This community is home to many vulnerable families, and it is here that Mercy Children's Home has set down its roots.
