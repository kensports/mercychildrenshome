About Us

Who We Are

Mercy Children's Home is a faith-based non-profit organization located in Kasanda Bukuya, Uganda, dedicated to providing comprehensive care for orphaned, abandoned, and vulnerable children.

  • Safe, loving residential care
  • Primary and secondary school education
  • Three nutritious meals daily
  • Medical and healthcare support
  • Spiritual guidance and mentorship
  • Vocational training for older children
Our Mission

What We Do

We provide holistic care that nurtures every aspect of a child's development — body, mind, and spirit.

Safe Shelter

A clean, secure, and loving home where children feel safe, valued, and protected every day.

Education

School fees, uniforms, books, and tutoring — every child gets the education they deserve.

Nutrition

Three balanced meals a day to keep our children healthy, strong, and ready to grow.

Healthcare

Regular checkups, vaccinations, and prompt medical treatment for every child in our care.

Spiritual Care

Faith, character, and values — we nurture the whole child through devotions and mentorship.

Future Skills

Vocational training and life skills to prepare older children for confident, independent lives.

Our Goal

Building God's Kingdom
One Child at a Time

"Train up a child in the way he should go; even when he is old he will not depart from it." — Proverbs 22:6. Our goal is not just to shelter children — it is to raise them in the love and knowledge of God, equip them with education and skills, and release them as God-fearing, productive members of society.

Protect Every Child

Provide permanent, safe homes for every orphaned and vulnerable child in our community.

Educate 100 Children

Ensure every child in our care receives a full quality education from primary through secondary school.

Raise Faith Leaders

Raise every child in the Word of God so they grow up to lead families, churches, and communities.

Expand Our Reach

Build a bigger home facility in Kasanda Bukuya to accommodate more children who need God's love.

Daily Life

A Place to Call Home

By the grace of God, every child who arrives at Mercy Children's Home finds more than a roof over their head — they find family, routine, love, and the presence of the Holy Spirit. We thank God for every generous heart that makes this possible.

Our Founder

Ssengonzi Alex

Born from a heart transformed by God's grace, Ssengonzi Alex founded Mercy Children's Home in Kasanda Bukuya, Uganda with a simple but powerful calling — to be the hands and feet of Jesus to the most vulnerable children in his community.

Having witnessed first-hand the suffering of orphaned and abandoned children in Uganda, Alex stepped out in faith in 2026 — with little more than a willing heart and the promise of God's provision. Today, by the grace of God and the generous support of friends around the world, Mercy Children's Home is a beacon of hope in Kasanda Bukuya.

"God called me to love these children as my own. Every child here is a miracle, and every sponsor is an answer to our prayers." — Ssengonzi Alex
