About Us

Who We Are

Mercy Children's Home is a faith-based non-profit organization located in Uganda, dedicated to providing comprehensive care for orphaned, abandoned, and vulnerable children.

Founded with a heart for the forgotten, we believe every child — regardless of background — deserves a safe home, quality education, proper nutrition, and a future full of possibilities.

  • Safe, loving residential care
  • Primary and secondary school education
  • Three nutritious meals daily
  • Medical and healthcare support
  • Spiritual guidance and mentorship
  • Vocational training for older children
Our Mission

What We Do

We provide holistic care that nurtures every aspect of a child's development — body, mind, and spirit.

🏠

Safe Shelter

We provide a clean, safe, and loving home environment where children feel secure and valued every single day.

📚

Education

Every child attends school. We pay school fees, provide uniforms, books, and after-school tutoring support.

🍽️

Nutrition

Three balanced meals a day ensure our children grow healthy, strong, and ready to learn and play.

🏥

Healthcare

Regular medical checkups, vaccinations, and prompt treatment ensure every child stays healthy.

✝️

Spiritual Care

We nurture children's faith, character, and values through devotions, mentorship and community worship.

🎓

Future Skills

Older children receive vocational training and life skills preparation for independent, successful adulthood.
