About Us

Who We Are

Mercy Children's Home is a faith-based organisation located in Kasanda Bukuya, Uganda, dedicated to providing comprehensive care for orphaned, abandoned, and vulnerable children.

We believe every child deserves more than survival — they deserve a future filled with hope, purpose, and opportunity. Through faith, care, and community support, we are helping these children grow into strong, confident, and God-fearing individuals.

But this mission is only possible with the help of people like you.

Our Mission

What We Provide Every Day

We provide holistic care that nurtures every aspect of a child's development — body, mind, and spirit.

Safe Shelter

Many of our children come from situations of neglect and uncertainty. Here, they find more than just a roof — they find a place where they are safe, loved, and finally at peace.

Education

Every child deserves a chance to learn and dream. We provide school fees, uniforms, books, and support — giving each child the opportunity to build a brighter future.

Nutrition

For many of our children, regular meals were never guaranteed. Today, we provide three nourishing meals a day — restoring their strength, health, and hope.

Healthcare

We ensure every child receives the medical care they need — from routine checkups to urgent treatment — so they can grow up strong and healthy.

Spiritual Care

Beyond physical needs, we nurture the heart. Through faith, love, and guidance, we help each child discover their value and purpose.

Future Skills

As they grow, we equip our children with practical skills and confidence — preparing them for independent, meaningful lives beyond the home.

Our Founder

A Heart for the Forgotten: Ssengonzi Alex

Ssengonzi Alex
Founder & Director — Mercy Children's Home

Ssengonzi Alex is the founder of Mercy Children's Home in Kasanda Bukuya, Uganda — a place where compassion has become a lifeline for vulnerable children.

His journey began with something simple but powerful: seeing children in his community going to bed hungry, without care, without protection, and without hope. Many were orphaned, abandoned, or living in extremely difficult conditions. While others passed by, Alex could not ignore their pain.

In 2020, with no funding and very limited resources, he made a life-changing decision — to open his heart and his home. What started with just a few children has now grown into a family of 32 children who depend on Mercy Children's Home for daily meals, shelter, care, and love.

Every day is a challenge. But despite the hardships, Alex continues to stand firm in his mission — to give these children not just survival, but a future. At Mercy Children's Home, these children are no longer forgotten. They laugh, they learn, and they begin to dream again.

"Every child you see here has a story — but together, we can help rewrite it with hope."

— Ssengonzi Alex, Founder
